## GENERAL PRODUCTION GUIDELINES

### Equipment Recommendations

**Minimum Setup:**
- Smartphone with good camera (iPhone 13+ or equivalent)
- Ring light or window light
- Simple background
- Caption app (CapCut, InShot, etc.)

**Professional Setup:**
- DSLR/mirrorless camera
- Wireless lavalier microphone
- 3-point lighting
- Professional editing software (Premiere, Final Cut)
- Stock footage subscription (Storyblocks, Artgrid)

### Stock Footage Sources

| Source | Best For | Cost |
|--------|----------|------|
| Storyblocks | B-roll, nature, lifestyle | Subscription |
| Artgrid | Cinematic, emotional | Subscription |
| Pexels | Free basic footage | Free |
| Unsplash | Still images | Free |
| Getty | Historical photos | Per-image |

### Music Sources

| Source | Best For | License |
|--------|----------|---------|
| Epidemic Sound | All genres | Subscription |
| Artlist | Cinematic | Subscription |
| YouTube Audio Library | Basic needs | Free |
| Musicbed | Premium | Per-track |

### Text/Caption Best Practices

1. **Readable on mobile** - Large enough without squinting
2. **High contrast** - White text with shadow/outline
3. **Brief** - 3-5 words max per frame
4. **Timed** - Match spoken words, slight lead time
5. **Consistent** - Same fonts/colors throughout series
