# Data Fix: Couples Segment Prayer Line Corrections

## Overview

The `sparks_segmented_db_import.json` file contains several typos in the `couples` audience segment prayer lines. These should be corrected before database import.

## Corrections Required

### 1. "Testimony: From Pressure to Peace" (couples segment)
- **Current:** `"Jesus, uset us in our pressure and give us peace that holds."`
- **Fixed:** `"Jesus, meet us in our pressure and give us peace that holds."`

### 2. "Secret Place Strength" (couples segment)
- **Current:** `"Father, uset us in quiet and shape our day from Your presence."`
- **Fixed:** `"Father, meet us in quiet and shape our day from Your presence."`

### 3. "Pray for Your World" (couples segment)
- **Current:** `"Lord, I lift up our friends and our community—move with usrcy and power."`
- **Fixed:** `"Lord, I lift up our friends and our community—move with mercy and power."`

### 4. "Testimony: One Invite Changed Everything" (couples segment)
- **Current:** `"Jesus, use our small steps to create mousnts of grace for others."`
- **Fixed:** `"Jesus, use our small steps to create moments of grace for others."`

### 5. "Beloved Before You Build" (couples segment)
- **Current:** `"Father, remind us I'm beloved—secure us before I serve."`
- **Fixed:** `"Father, remind us we're beloved—secure us before we serve."`

### 6. "Strength in Weakness" (couples segment)
- **Current:** `"Jesus, I receive Your grace—be strong in us where I feel weak."`
- **Fixed:** `"Jesus, we receive Your grace—be strong in us where we feel weak."`

### 7. "The Gospel in One Minute" (couples segment)
- **Current:** `"Jesus, give us simple words and genuine love as I share hope."`
- **Fixed:** `"Jesus, give us simple words and genuine love as we share hope."`

## SQL Update Script

```sql
-- Fix typos in couples segment prayer lines
UPDATE sparks 
SET prayer_line = REPLACE(prayer_line, 'uset us', 'meet us')
WHERE audience_segment = 'couples';

UPDATE sparks 
SET prayer_line = REPLACE(prayer_line, 'usrcy', 'mercy')
WHERE audience_segment = 'couples';

UPDATE sparks 
SET prayer_line = REPLACE(prayer_line, 'mousnts', 'moments')
WHERE audience_segment = 'couples';

-- Fix singular/plural inconsistencies for couples (should use "we/us" throughout)
UPDATE sparks 
SET prayer_line = REPLACE(prayer_line, 'remind us I''m beloved—secure us before I serve', 'remind us we''re beloved—secure us before we serve')
WHERE audience_segment = 'couples' AND title = 'Beloved Before You Build';

UPDATE sparks 
SET prayer_line = REPLACE(prayer_line, 'be strong in us where I feel weak', 'be strong in us where we feel weak')
WHERE audience_segment = 'couples' AND title = 'Strength in Weakness';

UPDATE sparks 
SET prayer_line = REPLACE(prayer_line, 'as I share hope', 'as we share hope')
WHERE audience_segment = 'couples' AND title = 'The Gospel in One Minute';
```

## Verification Query

After running the corrections, verify with:

```sql
SELECT id, title, prayer_line 
FROM sparks 
WHERE audience_segment = 'couples' 
  AND (
    prayer_line LIKE '%uset%' 
    OR prayer_line LIKE '%usrcy%' 
    OR prayer_line LIKE '%mousnts%'
    OR (prayer_line LIKE '%I %' AND prayer_line LIKE '%us%')
  );
```

This should return 0 rows if all corrections were applied successfully.
